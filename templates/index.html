Welcome to my Personal Website!

Greetings! My name is Chung Cheuk Yin, but most people call me Rico. I am an undergraduate studying Mathematics at the University of Oxford, Exeter College. Originally from Hong Kong, I’m passionate about understanding the world through the lens of mathematics and its incredible applications in both science and technology.

Alongside my studies, I work as a part-time tutor for Mathematics and Physics. Teaching has deepened my understanding of these subjects and fueled my desire to make education more accessible and engaging. I’m currently involved in a startup called Eddie, which seeks to revolutionize the tutoring industry by offering tools like a question bank, an online whiteboard, and a platform to connect tutors with tutees.

While academics play a big role in my life, I’m also someone who thrives on diverse experiences. I’m a keen fencer and basketball player, both of which challenge me to think strategically and stay active. Additionally, I’m learning the traditional Chinese unicorn dance, which connects me to my cultural roots and provides a fun, creative outlet.

This website serves as my platform to share my projects, interests, and journey as I grow academically, professionally, and personally. Whether it's a new mathematical concept, an AI project, or an update on Eddie, you’ll find it all here.

Feel free to explore the different sections of my website, and if you’d like to reach out, you can find my details on the Contact Me page. Your presence and feedback mean a lot—thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy your time here!
